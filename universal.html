Universal Studios Singapore

About the site

Universal Studios Singapore is Southeast Asia's first Hollywood movie theme park, located on Sentosa Island. It features thrilling rides, entertaining shows, and themed zones like Jurassic Park, Ancient Egypt, and Hollywood Boulevard.

Its history

Opened in 2010, Universal Studios Singapore is part of Resorts World Sentosa. It was built to attract global tourists and provide a unique entertainment experience in Asia.

  • How to get there:
    1. Sentosa Express - Waterfront station
    2. Taxi to Resorts World Sentosa
  • What to do:
    1. Enjoy rides like Battlestar Galactica
    2. Watch live performances
    3. Explore the themed zones
  • Time required: 6-8 hours
  • Best time to visit: Weekdays to avoid crowds
  • Opening hours: 11 AM to 6 PM
