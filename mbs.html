Marina Bay Sands

About the site

Marina Bay Sands is an iconic integrated resort located in Singapore, known for its stunning architecture and luxurious amenities. It features a distinctive three-tower design connected by a sky-high infinity pool, offering breathtaking views of the city skyline. The resort houses a hotel, a casino, shopping malls, world-class dining, and entertainment options, making it a must-visit destination.

Its history

Opened in 2010, Marina Bay Sands was developed by Las Vegas Sands as a part of Singapore's vision to become a global hub for business and tourism. Designed by architect Moshe Safdie, the project became one of the most expensive standalone casino properties ever built, costing approximately $5.5 billion.
