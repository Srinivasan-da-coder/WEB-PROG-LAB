Sentosa Island

About the site

Sentosa Island is a popular resort destination in Singapore, offering a variety of attractions, beaches, and entertainment. It features Universal Studios, S.E.A. Aquarium, Adventure Cove Waterpark, and beautiful sandy beaches, making it ideal for families and thrill-seekers alike.

Its history

Once a British military base, Sentosa was transformed into a tourist destination in the 1970s. Its name, which means "peace and tranquility" in Malay, reflects its serene beaches and scenic landscapes.

  • How to get there:
    1. Sentosa Express from VivoCity
    2. By cable car from Mount Faber
    3. Taxi or car via the causeway
  • What to do:
    1. Relax on Siloso Beach
    2. Visit Universal Studios Singapore
    3. Enjoy water activities at Adventure Cove
  • Time required: Full day
  • Best time to visit: Morning to evening
  • Opening hours: Varies by attraction
