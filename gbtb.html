Gardens by the Bay

About the site

Gardens by the Bay is a sprawling futuristic park in Singapore, renowned for its iconic Supertree Grove and biodomes like the Flower Dome and Cloud Forest. It showcases stunning displays of flora from around the world and offers breathtaking light shows in the evening.

Its history

Opened in 2012, Gardens by the Bay was developed as part of Singapore's initiative to transform itself into a "City in a Garden." It was designed to raise the quality of life by enhancing greenery and flora in the city.

  • How to get there:
    1. MRT - Bayfront station
    2. Bus - Gardens by the Bay stop
    3. Taxi - Stand near the entrance
  • What to do:
    1. Explore Supertree Grove
    2. Walk the OCBC Skyway
    3. Visit the Cloud Forest
  • Time required: 2-3 hours
  • Best time to visit: Evening for the light shows
  • Opening hours: 5 AM to 2 AM
