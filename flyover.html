Singapore Flyer

About the site

The Singapore Flyer is a giant observation wheel offering panoramic views of Singapore's skyline, Marina Bay, and nearby landmarks. It's a must-visit for breathtaking cityscapes, especially during sunset.

Its history

Opened in 2008, the Singapore Flyer was the world's tallest Ferris wheel at the time. It was built to boost tourism and provide a unique aerial view of the city.
